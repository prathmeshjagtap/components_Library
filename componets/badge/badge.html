Badges

Badge are used to highlight an item's status such as online / offline status , display numbers. Badges are small circles, positioned either at top-right or bottom-right of the parent component.

Badges on Avatar

Badges on avatar can be used to show online offline dnd status of the users, use class badge to form a badge and additionally you can use online offline and dnd classes to show the status

Badges on Different Positions

Badges can be placed on different positions by using classes such as top-left , top-right , bottom-right , bottom-left.

Icon Badges

You can add badges on Icons use class badge_count to show the count of notifications Imp : you cannot use postion classes for the icons it will be added soon
